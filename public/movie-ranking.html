Luke's Movie Review!

My top THREE movies of ALL TIME...(so far)

Cars (2006)

A fan favourite through my childhood years. A great soundtrack and some amazing story, and a nostalgia trip to say the least. What's not to love?

Hot Fuzz (2007)

One that I learnt about later in life, but I love all the same. Filled with endless laughs and timeless references, It's one I can't help but quote on a daily basis.

Good Will Hunting (1997)

Again, one that I found as I got older, but nonetheless shot up the movie rankings immediately. A mixture of laughs, pure emotion and the legend that is Robin Williams, it just had to make the list. How do you like them apples?
